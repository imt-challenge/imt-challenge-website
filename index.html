IMT Challenge

IMT Challenge is a cutting-edge training tool designed to help incident management team (IMT) members advance their planning and operational skills. By offering a variety of realistic scenarios that utilize land, air, and sea assets, IMT Challenge provides a comprehensive and immersive training experience.

With IMT Challenge, teams face dynamic situations in real time, requiring them to develop and execute effective strategies while adapting to evolving conditions. This tool is perfect for honing quick thinking, coordination, and decision-making abilities, ensuring that IMT members are prepared for any challenge.
